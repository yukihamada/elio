--- What Can MCP Do? ---

Calendar: "Show me today's schedule" - Auto-displays events
Reminders: "Remind me to take out trash at 10am" - Auto-creates
Contacts: "What's Suzuki's phone number?" - Searches contacts
Location: "Where am I?" - Gets current location
Photos: Attach image and ask "What is this?" - AI analyzes
Web Search: "Find latest iPhone prices" - Anonymous DuckDuckGo search
